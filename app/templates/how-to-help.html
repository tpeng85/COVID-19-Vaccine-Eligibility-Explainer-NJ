How to help

We are a group of volunteers who want to get New Jerseyans accurate information on the vaccine as fast as we can. Right now, we need more volunteers to get accurate information on vaccine availability in New Jersey. If you'd like to help out, please contact us as soon as you can!

Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

How can I help with this effort?

  • Get the word out about the vaccine and its availability.
  • Get the word out about this website and that anyone in NJ can find their eligibility information quickly here.
  • Email us at contact@covid19vaccinestatusnj.com to help with keeping track of vaccine availability.

Is the information on this website accurate?

We publish information that we receive as soon as we can. Things might change but we'll do our best to stay on top of them as they do. If something is incorrect on this website, please get in touch as soon as you can and we'll fix it!
