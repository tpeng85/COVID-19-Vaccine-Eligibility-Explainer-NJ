How pre-registration works

All New Jerseyans can pre-register for a vaccine right now, even if you're not eligible for a vaccine right now. You can pre-register for one on the official NJ Vaccine Scheduling System.

The pre-registration form will have questions like the ones on our questionnaire. When you finish pre-registering, you will get an email with your phase.

If you just want a quick answer and don't want to sign up for anything right now, you can take our questionnaire which will take you less time.
