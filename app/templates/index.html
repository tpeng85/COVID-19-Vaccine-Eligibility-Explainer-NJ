New Jersey Vaccine Information


If you're an adult who lives, works, or studies in New Jersey, you may be able to receive a COVID-19 vaccine from the government of New Jersey for free, whether or not you have insurance.

The current plan is to offer the vaccine in phases so that high-priority groups can receive the vaccine sooner.

This website serves as a short summary of the current guidelines for vaccine prioritization. To find out if you're currently eligible to receive a vaccine, simply click on the button below and answer a few yes or no questions. It only takes one to two minutes!




All New Jerseyans can pre-register for the vaccine, no matter what phase they are. Click here to find out more!

Current vaccine rollout phases: 1A and certain parts of 1B (Last updated January 18, 2021)
