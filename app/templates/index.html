New Jersey Vaccine Information


If you're an adult who lives, works, or studies in New Jersey, you will be eligible for the COVID-19 vaccine. Here on this website, it'll take you less than a minute to find out when you can get it!

The plan is to give the vaccine in phases so high-priority groups can get the vaccine sooner. But it can be hard to find your phase because official government announcements can be long and complicated.

We read the fine print so that you don't have to.

All you need to do is click the button below!

Remember: the vaccine is free, whether or not you have insurance!




All New Jerseyans can pre-register for the vaccine, no matter what phase they are. Click here to find out more!

Current vaccine rollout phases: 1A and certain parts of 1B (Last updated January 18, 2021)
